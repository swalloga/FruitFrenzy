How to Play:

  • The goal of the game is to line up three or more fruits
  • Click and drag to swap adjacent fruits
  • Clustered fruits will magically disappear from the game board. Where do they go? Maybe they're sangria now?!
  • Keep playing until you run out of valid swaps
